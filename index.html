FAQ

What are the fees for participating on easefees platform?
As supporters you can have access to private information of the needy. As regular members you have access to complete details on students profile. You can support them financially or mentor them directly with paid membership. As student you dont have to pay any fees.
Who is supporter or backer?
They support the student in need, either financially or with advice.
